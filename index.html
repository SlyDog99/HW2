About Sly

I was born in Tarnow, Poland on April 28, 1993. I have one simbling that is 8 years older than me. I moved to the United States when I was 10 years old. During the first few years we moved around a lot. We ended up moving to Buffalo Grove, Illinois and we still live there to this day. I went to Buffalo Grove High School and graduated in 2012. I then decided to attend a community college for 2 years before transferring to the University of Iowa.

I figured out that Business Analytics was my passion when I was completing my first semester at Iowa. After completing an internship this past summer, I was sure that analytics is what I want to do for a long time.
